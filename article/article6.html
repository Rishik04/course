7 Study Tips to Retain Information in Less Time!

Last-minute study? We all have been in that place. It feels so stressful and hopeless! But, don’t you worry, Notes Pickup has not only one or two, but 7 tips to retain information in less time while studying. Which will help immensely during the exam time?
The tips are as follows–


1. Sleep


Sleep! Yes, that’s right, that’s the tip guys. Sleep, drop your books and take a power nap of an hour or half, and then continue studying. Researchers say that proper sleep helps best in retaining information. When compared with students who were awake the whole night, students who slept the night were more prone to recalling complete information than ones who were awake the whole night studying.
So, during exams, it’s very important to indulge yourself in that beauty sleep!


2. Understand what you Study


It is very important to understand what you are studying. Simply reading something or memorizing something without understanding anything can lead to loss of information. If what you study has some kind of meaning in your memory you’ll definitely be able to recall it more often.


3. Reciting Loudly.


As loudly as possible! It is very important to hear what you are studying because then it easily gets recorded in your memory in no time. When last-minute studying, you don’t have time and are only reading the chapters to get an idea. Read loudly, it will help, trust me it will!


4. Keywords.


Find the keywords in your notes. Finding the keywords helps to remember the crust of the material. Even if you have forgotten what you studied under a particular topic. At least you will remember the keywords, around which you could build your answers.


5. Revise Highlighted Portions.


Rather than always reading the whole text while revising. Make it quick and study only the highlighted portions of notes. This will not only help revise in less time but also retainment of information effectively.


6. Listening to notes on a recorder.


To speed up the learning process effectively. You can make voice recordings of your notes in your phone. And listen to it repeatedly. Just like the way we learn the lyrics of a song after listening to it repeatedly, the same way you’ll notice that you have also learned your notes in no time.
So, what are you waiting for plug in your earphones!


7. Rote Memorization = Danger


Less time to study? Avoid rote memorization all together. When you are in for last-minute study never take the path of rote memorization. It will be a sure blunder. Too much information in your brain at the last minute will cause an immense amount of forgetting during the examination. Hence, as mentioned during last-minute studies understanding and learning is the best option ever. All these tips can be very handy if used wisely.
