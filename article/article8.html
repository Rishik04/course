7 TIPS TO MAKE YOUR NOTES MORE UNIQUE THAN OTHERS

AHEM-AHEM...ATTENTION

You must have always heard that learning is productive. But let us tell you, notes are equally profitable as well. To make your understanding effective, you need to have effective notes as well. So, why not make your notes more unique and well-organized than others so that whenever you open them again, you get impressed by yourself all over again. Let us have a quick glance at the ways through which you can make your notes look more pretty and unique.


1. AVOID FILLERS

Do not write unnecessary things. You do want to write extra and extra. In short, cut the crap. Keep the content short and crisp. By short, we do not mean that you stop writing even the main points. So, make sure that you write all those points which will help you to understand the content in the shortest way possible.


2. RECORD YOUR CONVERSATION


The reasonable way to complete your notes is to record the conversation that occurred during the classroom learning process. After that, refine it after the classroom session. Cut the unnecessary part and make only those points well-organized which are super-important and pass the criteria of need-to-know basis.


3. NOTE SHEET


There are some points which need some additional emphasis. For instance, while reading modern history, you need to emphasize the movements along with their year. So, such points require extra attention. You can give a shout out in such points by highlighting them either through highlighter or writing those points with a varied ink.


4. EXTRA ATTENTION, EXTRA SHOUTOUT


There are some points which need some additional emphasis. For instance, while reading modern history, you need to emphasize the movements along with their year. So, such points require extra attention. You can give a shout out in such points by highlighting them either through highlighter or writing those points with a varied ink.


5. OODLE-DOODLE


An elegant heading makes the topics nicer. Sometimes doodling with the topics makes things more interesting. Do not forget to bold the headings as the bold part gets imprinted into the mind very quickly. Some doodles in the topic retain the interest in the chapter as well.


6. STICKY IS SERIOUS


Sometimes, you forget to highlight the points or get to know some extra information about the topic sometime later. In that case, sticky notes save you the way. Easy to remove and stick to extra serious points, the sticky notes are crucial to point savers for learners like you.


7. TABLE IN THE FRONT


A table of content makes the notes look more professional. How many of the students actually make this? Well, not much. This is what makes it look different. A proper table of content helps you to know where to reach directly. It creates the surface gist of the chapter and helps the notes to look like a personal book, written in your own words, and at your convenience.
